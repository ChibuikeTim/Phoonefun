Our services

Device Unlocking

Nowadays most carriers will allow you to unlock your device by meeting certain criteria, whether it is paying off the device in full or reaching a set amount of days being active on their service. There may be situations where you need your device unlocked sooner than your previous carrier’s criteria. If you’re planning to travel internationally, looking to switch carriers, or whatever the reason may be – we can help!

Logic board and specialized repairs

With multiple certifications and plenty of experience in micro soldering and small component logic board repairs, our technicians can fix even some of the most complex issues that are causing devices to deadline. Should the repair prove to be ineffective, our background in data recovery can ensure that all your personal information may still be salvageable.

Breakdown or frequent bugs

Frequent bugs or software viruses can cause your mobile to run slowly or switch off completely.We can update your software or help you back up your data and restore your data to run as good as new.
